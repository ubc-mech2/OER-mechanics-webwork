This work was completed on the traditional, ancestral, and unceded territory of many peoples - specifically the Musqueam (xʷməθkʷəy̓əm), Squamish (Sḵwx̱wú7mesh), Tsleil-Waututh (səl̓ilwətaɁɬ), QayQayt, Kwikwetlem, Kwantlen, Ktunaxa, Tsawwassen (sc̓əwaθenaɁɬ təməxʷ), Chemainus (Stz'uminus), Stó:lō (S’ólh Téméxw), Kwanlin Dün, Niitsitapi (Blackfoot), and the peoples of the Treaty 6 and Treaty 7 regions of Alberta.
